What is Simple Invoices?

Simple Invoices is a free, open source, web based invoicing system that you can use from our server. It easily tracks your finances, send invoices as PDF's and hundreds of other great features.

How can you get it?

All you need to do is send an email to info@fs.al including your name/company's name and your monthly/annual subscription. Shortly you will receive a confirmation email with your account's username and password, as well as the URL of the service.
